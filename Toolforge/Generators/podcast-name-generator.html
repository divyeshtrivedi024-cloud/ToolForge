How to Choose a Binge-Worthy Podcast Name

01. Niche Focus Be specific. 'The Tech Podcast' is boring; 'Silicon Valley Secrets' is a brand.
02. Availability Check if the social handles and domain are available before committing.
03. Audience Connection Use words that resonate with your target listener's emotions or goals.
