Mastering Fantasy Character Naming

A name is a character's first impression. In high-fantasy settings like Dungeons & Dragons or Lord of the Rings, names often follow linguistic patterns. Our AI respects these traditions to ensure your character fits perfectly into the world.

01. Phonetic Consistency Elven names use soft vowels (A, E, I) and liquids (L, R, S), while Dwarven names use hard stops (K, G, D, T).
02. Surnames Matter Surnames like "Stonefist" or "Brightleaf" tell a story about the character's lineage or achievements.
03. Say it Aloud If a name is hard to say during an intense battle scene, it's probably too complicated.
