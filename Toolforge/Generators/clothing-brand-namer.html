Building a Fashion Empire

01. Phonetic Luxury Luxury brands often use short, sharp names (Gucci, Prada). Streetwear uses bold, descriptive words (Off-White, Supreme).
02. Visual Balance Think about how the name looks on a label. Symmetry in letters (like 'O' or 'X') can make a logo design much easier.
03. Cultural Resonance Ensure your name doesn't have an unintended meaning in other languages if you plan to go global.

Fashion FAQ

Do I need to trademark my brand name? Once you start selling, it is highly recommended to look into trademarking to protect your brand identity and logo.
