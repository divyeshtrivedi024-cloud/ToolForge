Growing Your Stream in 2026

01. Interactive Titles Titles that include [Sub Goal] or [Viewer Games] have a 40% higher click rate than generic ones.
02. Be Specific Instead of 'Playing Valorant,' try 'Road to Radiant - Game 1/10.' Progress attracts viewers.
03. Capitalize Key Words Using ALL CAPS for one or two words helps your title pop against the dark Twitch UI.
