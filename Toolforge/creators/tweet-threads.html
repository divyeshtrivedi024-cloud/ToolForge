Mastering the Art of the Thread

01. The Hook is Everything If the first tweet doesn't stop the scroll, the other 10 tweets don't matter. Use a 'Curiosity Gap'.
02. Use White Space Twitter is for fast reading. Avoid massive paragraphs. One sentence per line is the gold standard.
03. The 'CTA' Ending Always end your thread by asking for a Retweet or a Follow to close the loop.
