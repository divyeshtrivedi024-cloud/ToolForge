How to Write Captions that Get Saves

01. The First Line Instagram hides the rest of your caption after two lines. Put your "Hook" right at the start.
02. Micro-Blogging Longer captions that tell a story actually increase "Time on Post," which the algorithm loves.
03. Call to Action Always ask a question! 'Left or Right?' or 'Tag a friend who needs this' doubles engagement.
