Why use a Sitemap.xml?

            
An XML sitemap is a roadmap of your website that leads Google to all your important pages. It can be good for SEO, as it allows Google to find your essential website pages quickly, even if your internal linking isn't perfect.

            
            

                

                    
Clean Indexing

                    
By providing a sitemap, you ensure that search engine bots spend their "crawl budget" on your most important pages instead of getting lost in redirects.

                

                

                    
Submission Ready

                    
Our file format strictly follows the sitemaps.org protocol, making it 100% compatible with Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools.
