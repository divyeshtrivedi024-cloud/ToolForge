What are Meta Tags?

        
Meta tags are snippets of text that describe a page's content; the meta tags don't appear on the page itself, but only in the page's source code. They are essential for SEO (Search Engine Optimization) as they tell search engines what your site is about.

        
        

            
Why is the "Description" tag important?

            
While the description tag doesn't directly influence rankings, it significantly impacts your Click-Through Rate (CTR). A well-written meta description acts as an advertisement for your site on the search results page.

        

        

            
Do I need tags for Social Media?

            
Yes. Tags like og:title and twitter:card ensure that when someone shares your URL, it appears as a rich card with a high-quality image and title, rather than just a plain link.
