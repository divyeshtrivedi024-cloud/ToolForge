Mobile Tester

Check if your site meets Google's Mobile-First standards.

Note: Some sites (like Google) block previews for security. Use this to test your own local or public projects.

Mobile-First Indexing

Google predominantly uses the mobile version of a site's content for indexing and ranking. If your text is too small to read or your buttons are too close together on a phone, your SEO ranking will drop even if your desktop site is perfect.

User Experience (UX)

Over 60% of web traffic now happens on mobile devices. A mobile-friendly site isn't just for Google; it's for your customers. High bounce rates on mobile tell Google that your site isn't helpful, which indirectly hurts your search visibility.
