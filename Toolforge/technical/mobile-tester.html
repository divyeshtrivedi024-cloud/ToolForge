What is Mobile-First?

It means designing for the smallest screen first and working upwards. This tool helps you ensure your "Core Web Vitals" are solid on handheld devices.

Why do some sites fail to load?

Sites use an X-Frame-Options header to prevent "Clickjacking." For security, browsers respect this. This tool is best for testing your own apps during development.
