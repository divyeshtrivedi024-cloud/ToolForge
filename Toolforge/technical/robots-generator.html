The Role of Robots.txt

A robots.txt file acts as a gatekeeper. While it doesn't provide security, it manages how search engines interact with your pages, preventing them from wasting resources on non-public data.

Where does it go?

Upload this file to your root directory (e.g., domain.com/robots.txt). It will not work if placed in a subfolder.

Blocking AI Bots

Modern developers often block GPTBot or CCBot to prevent their content from being used in LLM training without permission.
