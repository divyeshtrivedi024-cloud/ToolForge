What is Structured Data?

Structured data (JSON-LD) is a standardized format for providing information about a page and classifying the page content. By telling Google "this is a tool" or "this is an FAQ," you enable enhanced features in search results, which can increase your click-through rate by up to 30%.

Software Schema

For websites like ToolForge, using **SoftwareApplication** schema is vital. It tells Google that your page isn't just a document, but an interactive tool. This can lead to your tool appearing with a "Rating" or "Price" (Free) directly in the search snippet.
