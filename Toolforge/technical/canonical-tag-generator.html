The SEO Power of Rel="Canonical"

Duplicate content is one of the most common reasons websites lose their rankings. When Google finds the same content on multiple URLs, it doesn't know which one to show, so it often penalizes both. A Canonical Tag acts as a "source of truth," ensuring all "link juice" goes to the correct page.

When to use it?

You should use this on every page of your site. It is especially important if you have tracking parameters (like ?utm_source=facebook) at the end of your URLs, as these create "new" URLs in Google's eyes that need to be pointed back to the original.
