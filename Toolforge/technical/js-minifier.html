Why Minify Your JavaScript?

Minification is a crucial step in the web optimization process. It removes comments, white space, and newline characters that are helpful for human developers but completely unnecessary for browsers to execute the code.

Does it change the logic?

No. Minification only changes the formatting. Your variables, functions, and logic remain identical, just packed tightly together.

How much speed do I gain?

On large projects, minification can reduce file sizes by up to 60-80%, leading to faster "Time to First Byte" and better SEO rankings for your site.

What about security?

Since this tool runs locally in your browser, your proprietary code never touches our servers. It is the safest way to minify sensitive logic.
