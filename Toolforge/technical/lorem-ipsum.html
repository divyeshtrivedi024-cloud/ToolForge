The History of Lorem Ipsum

Lorem Ipsum has been the industry's standard dummy text ever since the 1500s. It is a piece of classical Latin literature from 45 BC, making it over 2000 years old. Using placeholder text allows designers to focus on visual elements rather than getting distracted by the literal content.

Is it real Latin?

Not exactly. While it looks like Latin, the words are often scrambled or modified to remove meaning. This ensures that people look at the layout, not the text.

Why use this for web design?

Using real text during the design phase can be distracting. Dummy text provides a realistic look at font weight, line height, and spacing without requiring a copywriter.
