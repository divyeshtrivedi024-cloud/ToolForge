Scripting FAQ & Strategy

What is 'The Hook'?

The first 3-5 seconds determine if a viewer stays or clicks away. Never start with 'Hey guys, welcome back'. Start with the value.

Why are intros so short?

Retention drops significantly after 10 seconds. Your intro should answer 'What am I getting?' and 'Why should I care?' immediately.

How do I rank in Search?

Mention your primary keyword within the first 30 seconds of your spoken script. YouTube's AI transcribes your audio for SEO.
