Scripting Strategy

What is 'The Hook'?

The first 3-5 seconds determine if a viewer stays. Never start with 'Hey guys, welcome back'. Start with the immediate value.

Why so short?

Retention drops significantly after 10 seconds. Your intro must answer 'What am I getting?' and 'Why should I care?' instantly.

Rank in Search

Mention your primary keyword within the first 30 seconds of your spoken script to help the algorithm index your content.
