Pinterest SEO & FAQ

What is 'Aesthetic' SEO?

Pinterest users search for 'vibes'. Use descriptive adjectives like 'Minimalist', 'Vintage', or 'Ethereal' to show up in related feeds.

Do hashtags work?

In 2026, Pinterest prioritizes natural language over hashtag clouds. Use 2-3 specific tags max; focus more on the description text.

How to get more 'Saves'?

Pins with a 'Call to Action' like "Save this for your next project" have a 3x higher save rate than those without.
