Caption Strategy FAQ

The First 3 Words

On most platforms, users only see the first 3 words before the 'more' button. Start with a hook, not an emoji.

Whitespace Matters

Break up large chunks of text. "Airy" captions are easier to skim and keep users on your post longer.

Engagement Triggers

Ending with a question increases comments by up to 40%. Always ask for your audience's opinion.
