Bio Optimization FAQ

The 'I Help' Formula

A winning bio clearly states: "I help [Target Audience] achieve [Result] using [Method]". Clarity beats cleverness every time.

Why use Emojis?

Emojis act as visual bullet points. They save character space and add personality without cluttering the text.

What is a CTA?

Always end with a 'Call to Action'. Tell users to "Grab the free guide" or "Click below to work with me" to drive clicks.
