Cracking the Algorithm

High Stakes = Clicks

People click on tension. Instead of 'My Morning Routine,' try 'I Lived Like a Billionaire for 24 Hours.'

Search vs. Browse

Decide if people are searching for this (How-to) or if they need to be tempted by a crazy title (Entertainment).

Consistency Wins

One high-quality video per week usually outperforms daily low-effort uploads. Focus on retention, not volume.
