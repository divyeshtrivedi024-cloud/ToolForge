Cracking the YouTube Algorithm

01. Think Thumbnail First Before filming, ask: 'Can I make a high-quality thumbnail for this?' If the answer is no, the idea might not work.
02. High Stakes People click on tension. Instead of 'My Morning Routine,' try 'I Lived Like a Billionaire for 24 Hours.'
03. Search vs. Browse Are people searching for this, or do they need to be tempted by a crazy title? Know your audience's intent.

YouTube FAQ

How often should I upload? Consistency is better than quantity. One high-quality video per week usually outperforms daily low-effort uploads.
