Podcast SEO & Growth FAQ

Do keywords matter?

Absolutely. Spotify and Apple Podcasts use description text for search. Ensure your core topic and guest name appear in the first 2 sentences.

What's a 'Hook'?

It's a bold statement that creates a curiosity gap. Start with a question or a shocking statistic to stop the listener from scrolling past.

Why add timestamps?

Timestamps improve user experience and allow Google to index 'Key Moments' in your podcast if it's also on YouTube.
