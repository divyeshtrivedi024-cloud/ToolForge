LinkedIn Growth Strategy

The 'Hook' Line

Your first sentence must stop the scroll. Use a controversial take or a strong result. LinkedIn cuts off text after 3 lines.

Formatting is Key

Avoid walls of text. Use 1-2 sentences per paragraph. White space makes your post "breathable" and easier to read on mobile.

When to Post?

Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday between 9 AM and 11 AM usually see the highest engagement from professional networks.
