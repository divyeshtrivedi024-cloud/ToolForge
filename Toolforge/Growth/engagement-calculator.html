Why Engagement Matters More Than Followers

In 2026, the algorithm prioritizes meaningful social interactions. A small account with 10% engagement is more valuable than a huge account with 0.5% engagement. Use our tool to audit your brand or potential influencers.

What is a "Good" engagement rate?

1%–3% is average. 3%–6% is high. Anything above 10% is considered viral/excellent for small to medium accounts.

How is it calculated?

We use the standard industry formula: ((Average Likes + Average Comments) / Total Followers) x 100.
