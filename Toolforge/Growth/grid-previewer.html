Does ToolForge save my photos?

No. We use client-side JavaScript (FileReader API), meaning your images never touch our servers. Your privacy is 100% guaranteed as everything happens in your local browser.

How do I create a checkerboard feed?

The checkerboard style involves alternating between two types of content (e.g., a photo, then a quote). Use this previewer to ensure you haven't broken the pattern before you post.

What is the best grid size?

While some use "puzzle grids," the standard 3-column layout is the most effective for mobile users. We focus on the high-performance 3x3 layout for maximum clarity.
