What is Keyword Density?

Keyword density is the percentage of times a keyword or phrase appears on a web page compared to the total number of words on the page. In the context of SEO, it’s a measurement used to determine whether a web page is relevant to a specific topic.

The "Golden Ratio"

Most SEO experts suggest a keyword density of around 1% to 2%. Going above 3% can lead to "search engine penalties," where Google assumes you are trying to manipulate the system rather than providing value to readers.
