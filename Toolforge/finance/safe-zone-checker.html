Why Safe Zones Matter

There is nothing more frustrating than a high-quality video where the main text is covered by a "Share" button or the user's name. Our Safe Zone Checker provides a 1:1 simulation of the TikTok and Instagram Reels interface, allowing you to optimize your composition before you export from CapCut or Premiere Pro.

The "Dead Zone" Rule

The bottom 20% and the right 15% of your video are considered "Dead Zones." Important visual information like faces, product names, or subtitles should never be placed here. Use our tool to find the "Visual Center" of your content for maximum engagement.

Does this work for both iPhone and Android?

Yes. While screen ratios vary slightly, the "Safe Zone" areas are standardized across platforms to ensure the UI doesn't interfere with the viewing experience.

What is the best resolution for short-form video?

Always export in 1080 x 1920 pixels. This ensures high clarity while maintaining the 9:16 aspect ratio required for the full-screen experience.
