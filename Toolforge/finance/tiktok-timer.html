Timing is Everything

On social platforms, every second counts. If your script is too long, the algorithm might cut off your Call to Action. Our TikTok Script Timer uses a high-accuracy WPM (Words Per Minute) calculation to ensure your message fits perfectly within the 15, 60, or 180-second windows.

Optimize for Retention

The average speaker talks at 130–150 words per minute. For high-energy videos, you might want to hit 160 WPM. This tool helps you trim the "fluff" so you can maintain a fast-paced, engaging delivery that keeps viewers from swiping away.

What WPM does this tool use?

We calculate based on 145 WPM, which is the "Sweet Spot" for educational and entertaining social media content. It’s fast enough to be engaging but slow enough to be understood.

How do I shorten my script?

Look for filler words like "actually," "basically," or "just." Removing these can often shave 2–3 seconds off a script without losing any meaning.

Can I use this for YouTube Shorts?

Absolutely. YouTube Shorts has a strict 60-second limit. We recommend keeping your script under 140 words to ensure you have time for a strong intro and outro.
