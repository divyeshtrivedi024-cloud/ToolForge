Fairness Guaranteed

Running a giveaway on Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube requires transparency. Using a manual "scroll and point" method can lead to accusations of bias. Our Giveaway Winner Picker uses a cryptographically secure randomization method to ensure every entry has an equal mathematical chance of winning.

Boost Your Engagement

Giveaways are the #1 way to increase follower growth quickly. By asking users to comment and then using a professional picker to announce the result on your Stories, you build trust with your community and encourage participation in future events.

How do I import comments?

Currently, you can copy and paste the list of names or handles from your creator dashboard or spreadsheet. We recommend using a line-break for each entry for the most accurate results.

Is the selection truly random?

Yes. We use the Math.random() function combined with a shuffling algorithm that rearranges your entire list before picking the top entry, ensuring a 100% fair outcome.
