Why REM over PX?

Pixels are absolute. REMs are relative. If a user changes their browser's default font size, REM units scale with them, providing a much better accessible experience.

What is the formula?

Simply divide your Target Pixels by the Base Font Size. (e.g., 32 / 16 = 2rem).
