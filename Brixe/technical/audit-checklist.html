Mastering Technical SEO in 2026

A technical SEO audit is the process of checking the technical aspects of your website's SEO. It covers crawling, indexing, and website architecture. Even with great content, a site with poor technical foundations will struggle to rank.

Core Web Vitals Google prioritizes "User Experience." Ensure your LCP (Largest Contentful Paint) is under 2.5 seconds.
Index Coverage Regularly check Google Search Console for "Excluded" pages that should be indexed.
Semantic HTML Use H1-H6 tags correctly. Never use an H2 just for styling; use it for hierarchy.
