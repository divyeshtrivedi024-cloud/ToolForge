Why Use Semantic Markdown?

Markdown allows writers and developers to focus on hierarchical content structure. Converting this to semantic HTML5 is essential for SEO, as headers (H1-H3) and lists communicate the importance of information to search crawlers.

Is this secure?

Unlike other online converters that send your text to a server, Brixe processes everything locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your machine.
