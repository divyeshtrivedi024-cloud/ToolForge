The Art of Liquid Branding

A great cocktail is 50% ingredients and 50% presentation—and the name is the first part of that presentation. Whether you are crafting a menu for a professional lounge or naming a signature drink for a wedding, the name sets the expectation for the flavor profile. Our generator analyzes mixology trends to provide titles ranging from the historically inspired to the modern and experimental.

01. Alliterative Appeal Alliteration (e.g., "Midnight Mule," "Basil Bliss") makes a name more memorable and pleasing to say when ordering.
02. Mood Association Use the time of day or atmosphere as inspiration. "Morning Mist" implies something light, while "Eclipse" suggests depth and potency.
03. Technical Teasers Including a hint of the garnish or method (e.g., "Smoked," "Infused," "Spiced") helps guests understand the complexity of the drink.
