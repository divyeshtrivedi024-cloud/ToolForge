The Art of Small Business Branding

Etsy is a visual-first marketplace, and your shop name is the textual companion to your photography. A great Etsy name should be easy to spell, memorable, and suggestive of the quality of your work. Our generator focuses on "alliterative appeal" and "sensory nouns"—pairing materials with emotional outcomes. We've curated a database of over 250 aesthetic descriptors to help you find a name that resonates with your target audience's lifestyle.

01. Alliteration is Key Names like "Paper & Petal" or "Salty Stone" are easier to remember because of the repeating sounds.
02. Avoid "Crafty" Clichés Move beyond just adding "Store" or "Shop." Use more evocative words like "Atelier," "Finds," "Goods," or "Mercantile."
03. Check Social Handles Ensure your name is available on Instagram and Pinterest, as these will be your primary marketing channels.
