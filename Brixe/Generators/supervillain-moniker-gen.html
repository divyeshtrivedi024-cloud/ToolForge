The Psychology of the Antagonist

A great villain name isn't just cool—it's a warning. In comic lore, monikers often highlight a fatal obsession, a tragic origin, or a terrifying power set. Our generator prioritizes high-impact adjectives and authoritative nouns to ensure your villain sounds like a credible threat to any multiverse. Whether they are a master of the shadows or a titan of industry, their name is the first weapon they wield.

01. Titles of Power Using prefixes like "Baron," "Doctor," "Lord," or "The" creates an immediate sense of unearned authority and ego.
02. Contrast Branding Some of the best villains pair something innocent with something lethal (e.g., "The Glass Widow" or "Clockwork Carnage") to create a haunting mental image.
03. Phonetic Harshness Use hard consonants like K, T, and V to make the name sound aggressive and difficult to ignore.
