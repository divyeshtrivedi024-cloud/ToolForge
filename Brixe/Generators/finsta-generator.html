01. Anti-SEO Avoid using your full name or birthday. The goal is to be hard to find via the global search bar.
02. Prefix Logic Adding "not", "actually", or "literally" before your name is the classic Gen-Z way to signal a private space.
03. File Formats Using suffixes like .png, .mp4, or .zip gives your account an "archival" feel, perfect for photo dumps.
