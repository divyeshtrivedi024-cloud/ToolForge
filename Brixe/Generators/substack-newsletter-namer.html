The Science of Newsletter Branding

On Substack, your publication name is your first impression in the inbox. It needs to signal exactly what the reader will learn while remaining broad enough to allow your content to evolve. Our generator focuses on "Compound Professionalism"—pairing abstract concepts with structural nouns to create a name that sounds established and subscription-worthy.

01. The "The" Rule Many successful newsletters use "The [Noun]" format. it provides an immediate sense of authority and definitive perspective.
02. URL Compatibility Substack URLs are formatted as name.substack.com. Ensure your generated name doesn't contain confusing double-letters or difficult spellings.
03. Value Signaling Use words like "Signal," "Dispatch," or "Report" to tell the reader they are getting curated, high-value information rather than random thoughts.
