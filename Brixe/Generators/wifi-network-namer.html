The Art of the SSID

Your WiFi name (SSID) is the digital porch light of your home. It’s the first thing neighbors and guests see when they open their settings. While "Router_2.4G" is functional, a custom name adds personality and can even act as a subtle deterrent. Our generator balances humor with utility, providing names that are easy to find in a list while expressing your unique vibe.

01. Avoid Personal Info Never include your full name, address, or phone number in your SSID. It’s better to be funny or abstract than to give away your identity to passersby.
02. Keep it Short Most routers have character limits, and shorter names are easier for guests to type correctly when searching for your network.
03. Separate 2.4/5GHz If your router doesn't auto-switch, use a naming convention like "Airstream" and "Airstream_Pro" to help you quickly identify the faster band.
