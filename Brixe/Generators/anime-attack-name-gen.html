The Language of Battle

In anime, an attack name is more than a label—it's a manifestation of the character's soul. From the rhythmic chanting of "Kido" to the high-impact kanji translations of "Breathing Styles," names must sound powerful when shouted. Our generator prioritizes dramatic imagery, high-energy verbs, and celestial descriptors to ensure your technique sounds legendary.

01. Numerical Scales Many iconic attacks use "Forms" or "Types" (e.g., "First Form" or "Zero Type") to imply a vast hidden repertoire of skills.
02. Combined Elements Don't just use fire; use "Nebula Flame" or "Frost-Bite Void." Combining opposing or abstract concepts creates a more unique identity.
03. Phonetic Punch Use words with sharp sounds like "Strike," "Burst," and "Slash" to give the name a physical impact.
