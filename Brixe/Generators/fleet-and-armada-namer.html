The Architecture of Command

A fleet name dictates the scale of your narrative. While a "Ship" represents an individual character, a "Fleet" represents a movement, a nation, or an extinction event. We've expanded our database to include over 200 descriptors ranging from maritime history to celestial mechanics. Strike groups favor high-action verbs, while Hegemonies use nouns that imply unyielding stability.

01. Operational Designations For realism, use numbers (e.g., "7th Battlegroup"). Our generator mixes these with thematic names for a "Hero Fleet" feel.
02. Collective Nouns Use diverse terms for the group itself: Armada, Flotilla, Task Force, Cluster, Expedition, or Phalanx.
03. Thematic Synergy If your ships are named after birds, your fleet should be a "Flock" or "Wing." If they are named after swords, use "The Armory" or "The Sheath."
