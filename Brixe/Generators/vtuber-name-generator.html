Crafting Your Virtual Lore

In the world of VTubing, your name is the cornerstone of your lore. It needs to be catchy enough for a debut stream while fitting the visual aesthetic of your 2D or 3D model. Whether you are part of a corporate agency or an independent "indie-v," a strong phonetic name helps your community (your "fandom") build a brand around you.

01. Surname First Many VTubers use the Japanese naming convention (Surname + Given Name) to fit the anime aesthetic of the industry.
02. Emoji Synergy Choose a name that has an obvious "Oshi Mark" (representative emoji) like 🦊 for a kitsune or 🌙 for a moon spirit.
03. Alliteration Names that roll off the tongue, like "Kuro Kirin" or "Momo Mochi," are statistically easier for viewers to remember and search.
