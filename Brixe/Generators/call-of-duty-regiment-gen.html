Deploy with a tactical unit name designed for the Warzone leaderboard and Ranked lobbies.

Commanding the Warzone Theater

In Call of Duty, your Regiment (or Clan) name is your squad's calling card. It needs to reflect your playstyle—whether you're a high-coordinated Mil-Sim unit or a fast-paced Ranked squad. Our generator utilizes military phonetics and Tier 1 operator terminology to ensure your tag looks professional in the killcam and authoritative on the social tab.

01. The Tag Factor When picking a name, consider the 3-5 character [TAG]. Short names like "VORTEX" easily become [VTX].
02. Tactical Phonetics Using words like "Bravo," "Sierra," or "Kilo" adds an immediate layer of authenticity to your group's identity.
03. Avoid Ban-Bait Activision's filters are strict. Our engine avoids blacklisted terms to ensure your regiment remains active and report-free.
