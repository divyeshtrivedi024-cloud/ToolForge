The Registry of the Stars

A ship's name is its soul in the vacuum of space. Military vessels often adopt names that project power and history, while exploration ships lean toward human achievement and celestial curiosity. Our engine uses an expanded database of naval terminology, astronomical phenomena, and mythology to ensure your fleet sounds authoritative across any quadrant.

01. Prefix Designation Use prefixes like USS, HMS, or SS to ground your ship in a specific faction's lore.
02. Class Identity Larger ships (Dreadnoughts) usually have nouns like "Audacity" or "Vengeance," while smaller fighters favor verbs like "Stinger" or "Interceptor."
03. Lyrical Names Some of the best sci-fi names are short phrases (e.g., "Long Night of Solace"). Try the Abstract mode for poetic, non-traditional results.
