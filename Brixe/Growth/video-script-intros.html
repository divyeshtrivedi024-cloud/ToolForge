Video Script Intros

2026 Engagement Meta: Hook them before they can even think about scrolling.

Scripting Strategy

What is 'The Hook'?

The first 3-5 seconds determine if a viewer stays. Never start with 'Hey guys, welcome back'. Start with the immediate value or a stakes-driven statement.

The 10-Second Dropoff

Retention drops significantly after 10 seconds. Your intro must answer 'What am I getting?' and 'Why should I care?' within the first two sentences.

SEO Spoken Keywords

YouTube transcribes audio for search indexing. Mention your primary keyword within the first 30 seconds of your script to help the algorithm rank your video.
