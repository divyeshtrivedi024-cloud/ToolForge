Cracking the Algorithm

High Stakes = Clicks

People click on tension. Instead of 'My Morning Routine,' try 'I Lived Like a Billionaire for 24 Hours.' High stakes drive curiosity.

Search vs. Browse

Decide if people are searching for this (How-to) or if they need to be tempted by a crazy title (Browse/Entertainment).

The Thumbnail Test

Before filming, design the thumbnail. If you can't visualize a clickable, simple image for the idea, the concept needs more work.
